Creative writing

A. Write the introductory paragraph that includes the following:

  1. Background information:

    states the previous information about the topic or a historical fact.

  2. Thesis statement:

    it states the main idea of the essay.

  3. Roadmap:

    This is last paragraph of the introductory, where it informs the reader how you will prove and support your thesis statement along the body paragraphs.

B. Write a series of body paragraphs as convinient to your essay question. The body paragraph must include:

  1. Topic sentence:

    this is the first section where you state the main idea of the first body paragraph.

  2. definition of the main idea:

    In this section, you give the definition of the main idea with its subtopics.

  3. Give examples and solutions to the main idea

    in this third section, you provide examples of the main idea you stated and its solutions if the examples are effects or consequences. if the examples are positive, you may further explain the positive uses of the stated examples.

  4. conclude your paragraph:

    In this last paragraph, you summarize your main idea starting with a transitional device. transitional devices

C.write a conclusion that summarizes what the reader has read. it consist of 3 components namely:

  1. Summary of the thesis statement:

    you must rephrase the thesis statement with fresh and deeper understanding.

  2. Summary of the main ideas and points:

    You must briefly summarise the content that you mentioned in your body paragraphs by highlighting the most important points made.

  3. include a clincher:

    you must write a phrase that leaves your readers to have something to think about

WARNING:

  • Do not introduce new ideas in your conclusion that was not discussed in the essay.
  • Do not express again, word for word what you have already mentioned in the essay.
